About Us

MedLattice is a cloud-based healthcare management platform designed for multi-clinic chains and medical networks. It offers centralized administration, real-time EMR access, and seamless patient referrals across locations. With integrated telemedicine, role-based access control, performance tracking, and multi-location appointment scheduling, MedLattice streamlines operations and ensures standardized care across all facilities.

1. Centralized Multi-Clinic Management with Seamless Patient Transfers

Enables unified control and smooth patient movement across all clinics in the network.

2. Cloud-Based EMR and Telemedicine Integration

Offers real-time data access and virtual care through secure EMRs and video consultations.
